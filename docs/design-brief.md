# Design brief

Visual thesis: a quiet forest operations desk built from warm paper, deep moss, and one controlled fox-orange signal.

Content plan: lead with the live suspicion ranking, support it with location and signal summaries, expose editable observations below, and keep the AI Worklog one tab away.

Interaction thesis: stage the workspace on entry, animate score bars whenever inputs change, and reveal editing controls without moving the reader away from the current report.

The public calculation seam is `calculateFoxReport(observations, parameters)`.

The public user seam is the visible browser interface: change data or weights, observe the recalculated leader, open the formula, and inspect the AI Worklog.
